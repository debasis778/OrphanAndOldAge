ABOUT US

Nee Nestham is an Orphan and Old age homes which is founded by Buchireddy Srikanth Reddy He has got inspired by his father in helping every old people and he started this work with helping old people and creating a place for old people and later he started helping orphan childrens with his team.

Nee Nestham is a small community which has started in an small village and Established on October 29,2012. Nee Nestham has given shelter to many homeless and needy people including old people and childrens.

Ne Nestham has also provided food, clothes and shelter to more than 210 old peoples and childrens in this home and always taken care of every individual as a family member.
